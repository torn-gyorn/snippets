How to enter

Send Gyorn some Xanax, your entries will automatically be counted and assigned ticket numbers by the bot. For the first few rounds, the maximum number of entries is 20. Do not send more than 20 Xanax per round. 

Rules and Setup

1 Xanax = 1 Ticket

Round Length = 72 Hours

Maximum 20 Tickets per round

Ticket numbers are randomly assigned from the pool

If a ticket is drawn and there is no winning number, the next round will begin and the prize pool will carry over until a winner is finally drawn. You may purchase up to another 20 tickets in the next round and all subsequent rounds.
